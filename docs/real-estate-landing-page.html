Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize the property listing fields?

Yes. The template uses clear variable names for beds, area, price, and location. You can add or remove fields to match your market — no code restructuring needed.

Does it work for commercial real estate?

Absolutely. Just swap residential labels (beds, baths) for commercial ones (sqft, zoning). The layout and lead capture form work for any property type.

How do I collect leads from the form?

The form posts to any endpoint you choose — your own API, Formspree, Netlify Forms, or any backend. Just update the action URL in the editor.

Can I add more listings later?

Yes. The listings are data-driven — just duplicate a listing card and update the details. No design work needed. The grid auto-adjusts.
